Neferkasokar

Neferkasokar was a little-known pharaoh of ancient Egypt's 2nd Dynasty. His name appears in some king lists, such as the Saqqara King List, but he is absent from others, like the Abydos King List and Manetho's account, leading to uncertainty about his historical existence and reign. His name is recorded on a single artifact, the Cairo Annals Stone, which suggests that his reign was relatively brief and perhaps marked by instability. Due to limited archaeological evidence, Neferkasokar's role and achievements as a ruler remain obscure, and he is often regarded as a minor or possibly even mythical figure in Egyptian history.
