Hudjefa

Hudjefa is a mysterious and poorly understood figure from Egypt's 2nd Dynasty. His name, "Hudjefa," meaning "missing" or "erased," is thought to be a placeholder used by later scribes, as no original name or detailed records of his reign survive. He appears in the Turin King List, a New Kingdom-era document, and is associated with a reign of unknown duration, often seen as a sign of political instability or lack of preserved historical records during the late 2nd Dynasty. Some scholars speculate that Hudjefa may represent a break in the dynastic sequence or a king whose monuments and inscriptions were deliberately destroyed or lost.
